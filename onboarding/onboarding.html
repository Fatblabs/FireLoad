Privacy and Bandwidth

Prefetching can make Firefox contact linked sites before you click. Blazing Fast increases the chance of early requests and bandwidth use. Cross-site document prefetching is off by default and must be enabled in Options.

Guardrails

  • FireLoad skips sensitive paths such as checkout, payment, logout, token, and session pages.
  • Blocked hosts let you exclude private apps, admin tools, or fragile sites.
  • Save-Data and page opt-out signals are respected by default.
